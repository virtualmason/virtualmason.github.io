Founders Sales Program

The first sales hire should be the founders of the company because no one else knows, loves or has as much invested in the product. You bring the product knowledge and I bring the process and together we will build an outbound sales blueprint that we will work before you invest in bringing in more salespeople and systems.

We will partner together to get an understanding of your ideal customer and what makes them tick. We will get the messaging together, build scripts, have demos flows down, cover self-motivation, emailing for sales, technology needed, answers to objections, understand how to close the sale, being resilient and the rest of a repeatable sales system. We will both make calls setting appointments, giving demos and closing deals together.

Once we know what works we will make sure it's all documented and you're comfortable with the sales process. At this point not only are you now in a better position to not only sell your product but to hire a sales team, train them, motivate them and have a better understanding of your business and how to fix sales process problems because you didn't just read about it, you participated in the process. This is way better then hiring a bunch of people and wasting money becuase you have no idea what works at this point and they won't figure out if you can't.
